Ayoola John-Muyiwa, also known as AJ, is a Nigerian-American designer and product leader.

AJ leads product at Faves, a Lightspeed Venture-backed social media startup. Before Faves, AJ was a product manager at Facebook, where he worked on Stories, Facebook Lite, and Novi (blockchain payments), among others. AJ is also the Founder and Creative Director at Astro Media Lab, a human experience design and technology studio based in Silicon Valley.

As a Keynote speaker and moderator, AJ has presented at LinkedIn, ExxonMobil, and South By Southwest, one of the world's largest tech & media conferences. AJ's work has also been featured by Google, Techpoint Africa, Webflow, Inside AI, The Baytown Sun, Noire TV, and many others.
